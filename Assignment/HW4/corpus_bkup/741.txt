board of commissioners dare county is a part of the albemarle commission regional council of governments lighthouses dare county is home to two popular lighthouses the cape hatteras lighthouse and the bodie island lighthouse there is also a beacon atop the wright brothers memorial a third lighthouse was built by the town of manteo and dedicated on september 25 2004 the roanoke marshes lighthouse is an exterior recreation of the 1877 screwpile lighthouse of the same name and is located on the manteo waterfront it serves as exhibit space for the n c maritime museum on roanoke island transportation
