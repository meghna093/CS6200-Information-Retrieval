appearance chronology  sonntag 1982 einfach weg 1984  hurricane was the austrian entry in the eurovision song contest 1983 performed in german by westend the song was performed eighteenth on the night following portugal's armando gama with esta balada que te dou and preceding belgium's pas de deux with rendez-vous at the close of voting it had received 53 points placing 9th in a field of 20 entries the song is an up-tempo number in which the band sings about being without the object of their affections and likens this condition to being in a hurricane it was succeeded as austrian representative
