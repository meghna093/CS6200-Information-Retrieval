asp?lang en&n da74fe64-1 the canadian hurricane centre chc is a division of the meteorological service of canada an agency of canada's department of the environment that advises canadians on the threat of tropical cyclones such as hurricanes and tropical storms founded in 1987 chc provides guidance to msc's weather centres in eastern and atlantic canada and is based in dartmouth nova scotia chc frequently consults with its united states counterpart the national hurricane center in miami to coordinate the tracks and positions of storms that pose a threat to canada history hurricane gloria
