north-westerly course through the tropical atlantic ocean caribbean sea and gulf of mexico before making its final landfall near the united statesmexico border at peak strength it passed near haiti causing hundreds of deaths and heavy damage after crossing the gulf of mexico allen weakened as it struck the lower texas coast causing high winds a significant storm surge and heavy rainfall which
