pacific studies astronomy east asian languages and literature asian studies comparative philosophy marine science second language studies along with botany engineering ethnomusicology geophysics law hawaii-west oahu in kapolei primarily serves students who reside on honolulu's western and central suburban communities the university of hawaii community college system comprises four community colleges island campuses on o'ahu and one each on maui kauai and hawaii the schools were created to improve accessibility of courses to more hawaii residents and provide an affordable means of easing
