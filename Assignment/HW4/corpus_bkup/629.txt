houston hurricanes fc is an american soccer club based in houston texas hurricanes fc was established in december 2012 when the league announced that team owner brendan keyes was moving his galveston pirate sc franchise to houston the hurricanes regular kit colors are green and white horizontally striped shirts with white shorts and socks which derive from the iconic celtic f c uniform history
